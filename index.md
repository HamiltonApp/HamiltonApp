# Hamilton Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Hamilton ("the app") is a read-only health data dashboard for Android.

## What Data Hamilton Accesses

Hamilton reads health and fitness data from Android Health Connect on your device. This includes metrics such as steps, distance, calories, weight, body fat, lean mass, heart rate, resting heart rate, sleep, blood oxygen, and any other data type available in Health Connect. Hamilton accesses this data solely to display it to you within the app.

## What Hamilton Does With Your Data

Nothing. Hamilton displays your health metrics on screen and caches them locally on your device for performance. That is the full extent of what Hamilton does with your data.

Hamilton does not:

- Transmit any data to any server, cloud service, or third party
- Include any internet permission in the Android manifest (it cannot connect to the internet)
- Collect analytics, crash reports, or usage telemetry
- Create user accounts or require sign-in
- Use advertising SDKs or tracking frameworks
- Write to or modify your Health Connect data (read-only access)

## Data Storage

Health data is cached in a local database on your device to reduce the number of Health Connect queries needed. This cache is stored in the app's private storage and is deleted when you uninstall Hamilton or clear the app's data.

## Data Sharing

Hamilton shares no data with any third party. Hamilton has no mechanism to share data — it has no internet permission and no export functionality that sends data off-device.

## Your Controls

You can revoke Hamilton's Health Connect permissions at any time through Android Settings or the Health Connect app. You can also toggle individual metrics on or off within Hamilton's settings. Uninstalling Hamilton removes all cached data.

## Children's Privacy

Hamilton does not knowingly collect data from children under 13. The app has no account system and no way to identify any user's age.
